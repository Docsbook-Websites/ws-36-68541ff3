# How to Efficiently Capture Meeting Notes with NotedAI

Follow these steps to use NotedAI for capturing meeting notes and tracking action items effectively.

## 1. Sign Up for NotedAI

- Visit the NotedAI website.
- Click on the “Sign Up” button.
- Fill in your details and create an account.
- Confirm your email address to activate your account.

## 2. Set Up Your Profile

- Log in to your NotedAI account.
- Navigate to the “Profile” section.
- Add your name, company, and role.
- Customize your notification preferences to receive updates on action items.

## 3. Integrate Your Calendar

- Go to the “Integrations” tab in the dashboard.
- Select your preferred calendar service (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.).
- Authorize NotedAI to access your calendar.
- Choose the calendars you want to sync for automatic meeting note capture.

## 4. Start a Meeting

- Schedule a meeting using your integrated calendar.
- Ensure that the meeting link is included in the calendar invite.
- At the start of the meeting, open NotedAI and select “Start New Meeting.”
- Choose the meeting from your synced calendar.

## 5. Capture Notes in Real-Time

- As the meeting progresses, use the NotedAI interface to take notes.
- Utilize the “Smart Summarization” feature to highlight key points.
- Mark important discussions and decisions with tags for easy reference.

## 6. Track Action Items

- After the meeting, review the notes captured.
- Identify action items and assign them to team members directly within NotedAI.
- Set deadlines for each action item to ensure accountability.

## 7. Share Meeting Notes

- Once you finalize the notes, click on the “Share” button.
- Choose the recipients from your contacts or enter email addresses.
- Include a brief message summarizing the meeting highlights and action items.

## 8. Review and Follow Up

- After sharing, monitor the progress of action items through the “Dashboard.”
- Use the “Follow Up” feature to send reminders to team members as deadlines approach.
- Schedule a follow-up meeting if necessary to discuss unresolved items.

## 9. Analyze Meeting Performance

- Access the “Analytics” section to review meeting effectiveness.
- Evaluate how many action items were completed on time.
- Adjust your meeting strategies based on insights to improve future productivity.

By following these steps, you can effectively use NotedAI to streamline your meeting documentation process, ensuring that you capture essential information and track action items efficiently.
