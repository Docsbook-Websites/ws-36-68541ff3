## What You'll Learn
In this tutorial, you will learn how to set up NotedAI, configure your account, and start capturing meeting notes effectively. You will also discover how to utilize intelligent summarization and action item tracking to enhance your productivity.

## Prerequisites
- A NotedAI account (sign up at [NotedAI.com](https://notedai.com))
- A compatible web browser (Google Chrome version 90 or later, Firefox version 88 or later)
- Access to a microphone for audio recording

## Steps to Get Started with NotedAI

1. **Sign Up for NotedAI**
   - Visit [NotedAI.com](https://notedai.com) and click on the "Sign Up" button.
   - Fill in your details and create a password. Verify your email address to activate your account.

2. **Log In to Your Account**
   - Go to the NotedAI homepage and click on "Log In."
   - Enter your credentials and access your dashboard.

3. **Set Up Your Profile**
   - Navigate to the "Profile" section in the dashboard.
   - Add your name, company name, and any relevant details that will help tailor your meeting notes.

4. **Connect Your Calendar**
   - In the dashboard, go to the "Integrations" tab.
   - Select your calendar service (Google Calendar or Outlook) and follow the prompts to authorize NotedAI to access your calendar.

5. **Configure Meeting Settings**
   - Access the "Settings" menu and choose "Meeting Preferences."
   - Set your preferred note-taking style and action item tracking options. Select whether you want summaries to include key decisions and next steps.

6. **Start a New Meeting**
   - Click on the "New Meeting" button in your dashboard.
   - Choose whether to record audio or import an existing meeting recording. If recording, ensure your microphone is working properly.

7. **Capture Meeting Notes**
   - During the meeting, NotedAI will transcribe the audio in real-time. Focus on the discussion while NotedAI captures the details.
   - After the meeting, review the transcription and make any necessary edits.

8. **Review Summaries and Action Items**
   - Once the meeting concludes, navigate to the "Meetings" tab.
   - Review the automatically generated summary and action items. Assign tasks to team members directly from this interface.

9. **Export or Share Notes**
   - Use the export feature to download your meeting notes in PDF or Word format.
   - Share notes directly with your team via email or collaboration tools like Slack.
